# India payments notes

NPCI, the National Payments Corporation of India, operates the Unified
Payments Interface along with RuPay and IMPS. UPI transfers money between
bank accounts within seconds at any hour, addressing payees by virtual
payment addresses so account numbers stay private. Between banks, NPCI
settles net obligations in scheduled batches even though users see instant
confirmation. Credit on UPI and autopay mandates are expanding the network
from payments into credit infrastructure.
